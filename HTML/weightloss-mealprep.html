Weight Loss Meal Prep

Delicious, calorie-conscious meals to support your fat-burning goals.

Grilled Chicken & Veggies

High-protein, low-carb plate perfect for lunch or dinner.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoon olive oli
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1 tablespoon paprika
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Pack of mix Veggies

Calories Per Serving: 350-400 kcal

Overnight Oats

Fiber-rich breakfast with chia seeds and almond milk.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1/2 cup rolled Oats
1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk or greek yogurt
1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional for fiber)
Fruits (banana, berries, nuts,) 120kcal

Calories Per Serving: 350-450 kcal

Quinoa Salad

Loaded with greens, chickpeas, and light vinaigrette.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1 cup quinoa (uncooked)
1 1/2 cup water or vegetable broth
1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 avocado, diced
1/2 cucumber, diced
1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoon olive oil
1 tablespoon lemon juice
salt, pepper, and parsley or cilantro for garnish

Calories Per Serving: 350-450 kcal
