Muscle Gain Meal Prep

Protein-rich meals to fuel growth and recovery.

Steak & Broccoli

High-protein and iron-packed combo to build strength.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
5 oz lean beef steak (sirloin or flank)
1 1/2 cup broccoli florets
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tablespoon soy sauce
salt and pepper to taste

Calories Per Serving: 400-450 kcal

Chicken & Brown Rice

Classic bulking meal with lean protein and complex carbs.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
4 - 5 oz grilled or baked chicken breast
3/4 cup cooked brown rice
1/2 cup mixed veggies
1 tablespoon olive oil
herbs and spices for flavor

Calories Per Serving: 420-480 kcal

Post-Workout Shake

Whey protein blended with banana, oats, and almond milk.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1 scoop whey protein
1 medium banana
1 cup unsweetened almond milk or regular milk
1 tablespoon peanut butter or oats
iced or water (optional)

Calories Per Serving:
With Almond milk: 300-350 kcal
With whole milk or peanut butter: 400-450 kcal
