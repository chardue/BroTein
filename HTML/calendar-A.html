14-Days Workout Plan

Day 1

Full Body Starter

Jumping Jacks: 30sec

Wall sit: 30sec

Push-ups: 10 reps

Glute bridges: 15reps

Plank: 20sec (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 2

Lower Body Focus

Bodyweight squats: 15reps

Step-ups (on stairs or chairs): 10 each leg

Calf raises: 20reps

Wall sit: 30sec

Side leg raises: 10 each side (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 3

Core & Abs

Crunches: 15reps

Leg raises: 10reps

Russian twists: 20reps (each side)

Planks: 30sec

Bird-dogs: 10 each side (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 4

Active Recovery

20-30 minutes brisk walking or light yoga/stretching

Day 5

Upper Body & Cardio

Arm circles: 1min

Push-ups: 10reps

Triceps dips (using a chair): 10reps

High knees: 30sec

Punches in place: 1min (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 6

Glutes & Legs

Donkey kicks: 15 each side

Fire hydrants: 15 each side

Wall sit: 40sec

GLute bridge: 20reps

Squats: 15reps (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 7

Rest or Gentle stretching

Day 8

Full Body Refresh

Jumping Jacks: 45sec

Push-ups: 12reps

Glute bridges: 20reps

Planks: 30sec (repeat 3 rounds)

Day 9

Lower Body Strength

Squats: 20reps

Step-ups: 12 each leg

Wall sit: 45sec

Side lunges: 10 each side (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 10

Core & Balance

Bicycle crunches: 20reps

Bird-dog hold: 10 per side

Planks: 40sec

Reverse crunches: 10reps

Side plank: 20sec per side

Day 11

Active Recovery

Stretch, Walk, or try a beginner yoga.

Day 12

Cardio & Coordination

High knees: 45sec

Arm punches: 1min

Mountain climbers (slow pace): 30sec

Jumping jacks: 45sec

March in place: 1min (repeat 2-3 rounds)

Day 13

Full Body Burn

Push-ups: 12reps

Squats: 20reps

Glute bridge: 20reps

Planks: 30sec

Jumping jacks: 1min (repeat 3 rounds)

Day 14

Celebrate with Movement

Pick your favorite exercises from previous days and create your own 20-minute flow. End with stretching or a walk.
