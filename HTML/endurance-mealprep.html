Endurance Meal Prep

Balanced meals to support long-lasting energy and stamina.

Whole Wheat Pasta

Carb-loaded meal with roasted veggies to fuel endurance.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1 cup cooked whole wheat pasta
1/2 cup roasted veggies
2 oz grilled chicken or lean sausage
1 tablespoon olive oil
Salt, pepper, garlic, and herbs to taste

Calories Per Serving: 400-500 kcal

Smoothie Bowl

Antioxidant-rich fruit bowl topped with oats & seeds.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1/2 frozen banana
1/2 cup mixed frozen berries
1/2 cup greek yogurt or unsweetened almond milk
2 tablespoon of granola

Calories Per Serving: 350-450 kcal

Eggs & Avocado Toast

Healthy fats and protein for sustained energy release.


Meal Prep Ingredients:
1 slice whole grain bread
1/2 ripe avocado
1-2 boiled or poached eggs
Optional: Chili flakes, lemon juice, olive oil

Calories Per Serving: kcal
